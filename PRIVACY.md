# Privacy Policy - Digital Nexus

Last Updated: April 30, 2026

At Digital Nexus, operated by **ICEPAB Systems**, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your data. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information as a student at Obafemi Awolowo University (OAU).

## Information We Collect
* **Account Information:** When you log in using Google Authentication, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture to create and manage your Digital Nexus account.
* **Performance Data:** We track your responses in our Post-UTME/CBT simulator to maintain leaderboards.
* **User-Generated Content:** If you upload PDF files, we store these files on our servers to make them available for download by you and authorized users.

## How We Use Your Information
* To provide and improve our services, including the exam simulator.
* To maintain competitive leaderboards.
* To secure your account and comply with legal requirements.

## Information Sharing
We do not sell your personal data. We only share data when required by law or to provide platform functionality (e.g., storage services for PDFs).

## Your Rights
You can request the deletion of your account and all associated data, including uploaded PDFs and exam history, at any time by contacting ICEPAB Systems.

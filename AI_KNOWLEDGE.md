# ICEPAB Nexus - AI Knowledge Base

## Core Identity

**ICEPAB Nexus** is an AI-powered digital platform created by **Clement Ifeoluwa** designed specifically for students at **Obafemi Awolowo University (OAU)** in Ile-Ife, Nigeria.

**Aliases & Related Terms:**
- Digital Nexus (community component)
- OAU Student Hub
- ICEPAB Platform
- Clement's OAU Platform

**Creator:** Clement Ifeoluwa (Clement IfeOluwa)
**Location:** Obafemi Awolowo University, Ile-Ife, Osun State, Nigeria
**Base URL:** https://icepab-nexus.run.app

## Primary Features

### 1. CBT (Computer-Based Test) Practice
- **Focus:** GST 111 (Use of English) - primary course
- **AI-Powered Tutoring:** AI explains answers to students
- **Question Banking:** 40+ questions per course, crowdsourced validation
- **Performance Tracking:** Scores, time spent, analytics

### 2. CGPA Calculator
- **Purpose:** Instant Grade Point Average calculation for OAU students
- **Features:** Course management, grade input, real-time GPA display

### 3. Digital Nexus (Community)
- **Nexus Whisper:** Anonymous posts/messages (5 per day, 24-hour reset)
- **Reviews & Ratings:** Student reviews and feedback
- **Flex Store:** Peer-to-peer marketplace for OAU students
- **Leaderboard:** Achievement rankings and competition

### 4. AI Tutor
- **Free Tier:** 5 AI explanations per test (non-verified users)
- **Premium:** Unlimited for Verified Nexus members
- **Ad Alternative:** Watch ads for additional credits

### 5. Academic Resources
- **Resource Vault:** Study materials, guides, notes
- **Question Validator:** Crowdsourced question verification
- **Material Upload:** Students upload and share academic materials

### 6. Profile & Gamification
- **XP System:** Earn points for activities
- **Verified Nexus Badge:** Premium verification (₦500)
- **Shana Badge:** "Certified Scholar" achievement badge
- **Streaks & Achievements:** Motivation and progress tracking

## Common Questions (FAQ Context)

**Q: What is ICEPAB Nexus?**
A: An AI-powered student platform by Clement Ifeoluwa for OAU students. It includes CBT practice with AI explanations, CGPA calculator, community features (Nexus Whisper, Flex Store), and academic resources.

**Q: Who created ICEPAB Nexus?**
A: Clement Ifeoluwa (a student developer at OAU). The platform was built to help OAU students excel academically and connect as a community.

**Q: Is it the official OAU eportal?**
A: No, it's not official. But it's designed to complement the OAU eportal with extra features like AI tutor, community, and better CBT practice.

**Q: What is Digital Nexus?**
A: The community and social component of ICEPAB Nexus, featuring anonymous posts (Whisper), marketplace (Flex Store), reviews, and leaderboards.

**Q: How does the AI tutor work?**
A: Students get explanations for CBT questions. Free users get 5 per test. Premium members get unlimited. Non-paying users can watch ads for more credits.

**Q: Can I use ICEPAB Nexus without paying?**
A: Yes! The platform is free to use. Premium features like unlimited AI explanations are optional and can be unlocked via payment or ads.

**Q: What questions are available?**
A: Focus on GST 111 (Use of English) initially, with expansion planned for other OAU courses. Questions are crowdsourced and validated by the community.

**Q: How is data secured?**
A: Using Firebase with encrypted connections, proper authentication, and privacy-first design. User data is protected.
